# DOOMGATE Image Prompts (Gemini-ready)

These prompts are written so you can paste them into an image generator (e.g., Gemini) to create **backgrounds** and **pixel sprites** that match the story.

## Global style constraints (apply to ALL images)

- **Aesthetic**: NES-era point-and-click adventure homage (Shadowgate), fused with **Doom/UAC industrial hell**.
- **Backgrounds**: static scene art, slightly painterly but *readable*, strong silhouettes, moody lighting.
- **Color**: UAC steels (cold gray/green), hazard reds, sickly argent greens, warm amber emergency lights.
- **No UI**: do not draw text boxes, buttons, UI frames, or captions in the image.
- **Camera**: fixed 2D viewpoint, like a painted background plate.
- **Aspect for backgrounds**: 2:1-ish wide, or 980×480 recommended.
- **Sprites**: pixel-art, **transparent background (PNG alpha)**, crisp edges, no anti-aliasing.

## Room background prompts (13)

### 1) Hangar Intake — The Crucible Facility (`hangar`)
Create a static background of a UAC hangar intake on Mars: ribbed steel arches and bulkhead ribs overhead, industrial floor plating, scattered crates and a dented supply crate in the foreground, a flickering wall terminal on the left emitting green phosphor glow, UAC logos partially defaced by etched demonic runes and claw marks, faint mist and dust motes in the light. Lighting: red emergency strobes + sickly green terminal glow. Subtle blood smears and scorch marks. Mood: tense, abandoned, “corporate facility built on a cursed ruin.”

### 2) Main Corridor — Steel & Sigils (`corridor`)
Long perspective corridor: brushed steel walls, conduit pipes, overhead panels, red emergency lights pulsing along the ceiling. Demonic glyphs crawling over UAC signage like graffiti that bites into metal. Floor shows a shallow pool of glowing green sludge near the center-right foreground (hazard). Doors at far end and side hall visible. Atmosphere: ozone, smoke haze, oppressive depth.

### 3) Security Checkpoint — Locked Teeth (`security`)
Security booth with shattered glass window, overturned chairs, smeared keypad panel, a reinforced weapons locker behind a barrier, a corpse in armor slumped near the right side, keycard lanyard visible. UAC caution stripes, flickering monitor glow. Blood handprints on the wall. Industrial steel + green/amber indicator lights.

### 4) Maintenance Ductworks — The Belly of UAC (`maintenance`)
Claustrophobic maintenance passage: thick pipes, steam leaks, grated floor, heavy conduit bundles. A power conduit column pulsing with argent-green energy near the mid-right. Dim lighting with green reflections. UAC access panel broken open. Mood: “machinery breathing.”

### 5) Research Labs — Blast Door (`labsDoor`)
A heavy blast door dominates the scene. The keypad area is defaced with a blood-painted demonic seal: circular glyphs, smeared runes, gore-red paint over circuitry diagrams. Steel plating meets ancient black stone seams beneath. Lighting: amber + red, with faint green reflections.

### 6) Research Labs — Argent Containment (`labs`)
Shattered lab benches, cracked containment tubes, broken glass, cables, warning lights. At center is a pedestal with a bright stable argent energy crystal (Omega Crystal) glowing cyan-green, casting hard-edged light and reflections. Background shows lab walls with UAC signage, some demonic scorch patterns. Mood: sterile + sulfur.

### 7) Living Quarters — Echoes of Payroll (`quarters`)
Bunks and lockers in disarray, a workplace safety poster torn and rewritten in blood, scattered personal items. A cracked wall panel partially pried open reveals a dark recess. Intercom speaker box on wall. Lighting: dim greenish ambient with red emergency glow; mood: lived-in, suddenly evacuated.

### 8) Service Lift — Down to the Ruins (`lift`)
Freight elevator interior fused with bone-white growths creeping along seams. Panel with only UP and DOWN indicators (no readable text, just lit buttons). Industrial cage, cables, worn metal. Lighting: sickly green + amber. Mood: “down is a dare.”

### 9) Excavation Hatch — Sealed (`hatch`)
Heavy EXCAVATION hatch door in a narrow metal corridor. Claw marks scored across the frame. Biometric lock panel glowing angry red. Dust, grit, faint demonic black stone behind seams. Lighting: red lock + dim ambient.

### 10) Excavation Site — Temple Breach (`excavation`)
Large excavation pit: UAC floodlights illuminating ancient black stone. A half-buried demonic arch/portal frame at the back. Dust and red haze. A Hell Knight silhouette patrols near the altar (no action pose needed, just looming). The altar has a small bright object notch (Neural Link). Mood: grand, dangerous, archeology-of-hell.

### 11) Hell-Gate Antechamber — The Threshold (`antechamber`)
Antechamber where UAC steel plating overlays ancient stone: big demonic door at north wall, serpent-themed lock at center. The stone is carved with faint runes, and cables snake along it. Lighting: green accent + amber rim light. Mood: threshold, dread.

### 12) Armory Annex — Old Toys (`armory`)
Small armory room with mostly empty racks and one intact display case on a pedestal containing a biomechanical demonic-metal key (Serpentine Key) faintly glowing red. Warning stripes, UAC steel, low lights. Mood: “museum for bad ideas.”

### 13) Hell-Gate Chamber — The Crucible (`hellgate`)
Massive chamber: half factory, half cathedral. Tall columns, industrial catwalks, cables, and ancient stone. A swirling red Hell rift at far right casting infernal light. Director Crux stands near the rift (humanoid silhouette fused with cables/robes/horns). Above the rift, an enormous Titan silhouette presses against reality like a shadow behind a membrane. Lighting: red rift glow + green industrial accents. Mood: final ritual, apocalyptic.
